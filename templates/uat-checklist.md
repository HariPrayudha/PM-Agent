## Aturan Main UAT (sampaikan ke client di awal — EN)

> "During UAT ({dates}), please test the flows below and log any issues in {ClickUp form / sheet}. We'll categorize each item as a **bug** (something not working as specified in the PRD — we fix it free of charge) or a **change request** (new/changed behavior — we'll estimate impact first). UAT feedback is due by **{date}**; items reported after that will be scheduled into the next cycle."

Kenapa aturan ini penting: tanpa batas waktu & definisi bug-vs-change, UAT jadi pintu revisi tak berujung → KPI low revision rate hancur.
